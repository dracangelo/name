Anime

Anime is a hand drawn and computeranimation originating from Japan. Anime is often characterized by colorful graphics, vibrant characters and fantastical themes.

Here are our top three animes to watch.

Tokyo Ghoul

In modern day Tokyo, society lives in fear of Ghouls- mysterious creatures who look like humans .... yet hunger insatiably for their flesh. None of this matters to Ken Kenaki, a bookish and ordinary man, until a dark and violent encounter turns him into the first ever Ghoul-human half breed. Trapped between two worlds, Ken must survive the violent conflicts of warring Ghoul factions, while attempting to learn more about Ghoul society, his new found powers and the fine line between man and monster.

Attack on Titan

Several years ago, humans were nearly eterminated by titans-tall creatures with no intelligencs that devour humans for fun-.A small percentage survived by enclosing themselves in a city that is protected by extremly high walls-taller than any titan. 100 years have passed with no titan in sight. Eren, a teenage boy and Mikasa, his foster sister together with Armin witness something horrific as a collosal titan destroy the city's first wall. As havoc reigns as the smaller titans flood the city, eveeryone is forced to flee. Eren and Micasa watch in horor as their mother is devored. They both vow to destroy all titans as vengence for mankind. Watch trailer

Death Note

Light Yagami is a 17-year-old genius from Japan who is tired of his life, school and stat of the worldas he knows it. One day as he walks home from class, he stumbles on a dark notebook with "death note" written on the front. After reading the first instructions he discards it as a joke. Light continues with his daily activities but soon enough his human curiosity takes the better of him and tries the notebook and discovers the truth of the first statement. Withnpower of death in his hands, Light Yagami is on a quest to change the world and become the god of the new world.
